Community Tips and Tricks

Welcome to the community forum! Share your best strategies, ask questions, and learn from experienced players.

Beginner Tips

  • Crosshair Placement: Always keep your crosshair at head level and pre-aim common angles
  • Economy Management: Learn when to save, force buy, or full buy with your team
  • Sound Cues: Use headphones and listen for enemy footsteps and reloads
  • Spray Control: Practice recoil patterns in aim maps and deathmatch
  • Communication: Make clear callouts and share information with your team

Intermediate Tips

  • Utility Usage: Learn common smoke, flash, and molotov lineups for each map
  • Trading Kills: Always trade your teammates - don't let enemies get free kills
  • Positioning: Hold off-angles and unexpected positions to catch enemies off guard
  • Game Sense: Read the enemy's economy and predict their strategy
  • Movement: Master counter-strafing to improve your accuracy while peeking

Advanced Tips

  • Timing Plays: Use delays and fakes to disrupt enemy rotations
  • Clutch Situations: Stay calm, use time wisely, and isolate 1v1 duels
  • AWP Positioning: Learn aggressive and passive AWP positions for different situations
  • Team Coordination: Practice site executes and retakes with your team
  • Demo Review: Watch your own demos to identify mistakes and improve decision-making
